TWIST
Interchangeable Circular Needles

TWIST interchangeable needles are the perfect choice for knitters who love to work with pointy, lace tips! Needle tips are made from lightweight surgical-grade, stainless steel. TWIST cords are multi-stranded steel cable coated in nylon with a smooth finish. These cords extremely flexible and memory free! Cord ends feature a lifeline hole -- great for lace knitters.

We carry a variety of interchangeable sets, in regular length tips and shorter tips to make 16" circulars. We also have individual needle tips (sold in pairs) and cords in a variety of lengths. NOTE: Small-size cables fit needle sizes US 2 through US 8 and large-size cables fit US 9 through US 15
