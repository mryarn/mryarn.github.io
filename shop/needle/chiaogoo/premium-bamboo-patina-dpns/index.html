ChiaoGoo
Premium Bamboo - Patina
Double-Pointed Needles

ChiaoGoo Premium Bamboo double pointed needles are crafted from Chinese Moso bamboo, one of the strongest bamboo species in the world. The tips of each needle are hand crafted to beautifully tapered points allowing for smooth knitting on all yarn types. The US and metric size is laser imprinted on each needle for easy identification that will not wear or fade. These needles offer great strength and flexibility while being extremely affordable -- great for supplementing your needle collection!

We offer individually packaged 6" and 8" sizes in a dark patina color as well as a 6" DPN Sock Set with case and accessories.

Premium Bamboo 6" Double-Pointed Needle Sock Set

This set of 6" double-pointed needles comes in a black and white print fabric zip case. Contains 5 of each of the following sizes: US 1 (2.25mm), US 1.5 (2.5mm), US 2 (2.75mm), US 2.5 (3mm), US 3 (3.25mm); 12 stitch markers; 5" needle gauge.
