Knitter's Pride
Karbonz
Double-Pointed Needles

Karbonz are a revolutionary product made with carbon fiber which is super lightweight, strong and flexible. The nickel-plated brass tips and smooth carbon fiber body allow your stitches to glide effortlessly across the needle. Karbonz come in a large selection of sizes from an amazingly small US 00000 (1mm) to US 10 (6mm). Note that sizes US 00000 and 0000 are extremely fine and do not have brass tips.

We carry individual sizes which come in sets of 5 needles and also a complete set of 6" length needles in popular small sizes, perfect for sock knitters.

Karbonz 6" Double-Pointed Needle Set

This set of 6" double-pointed needles comes in a black and grey fabric case with a clear window. Contains 5 of each of the following sizes: US 0 (2mm), US 1 (2.25mm), US 1.5 (2.5mm), US 2 (2.75mm), US 2.5 (3mm), US 3 (3.25mm).
