Knitter's Pride
Karbonz
Interchangeable Circular Needles

Karbonz are a revolutionary product made from carbon fiber which is super lightweight, strong and flexible. The nickel-plated brass tips and smooth carbon fiber body allow your stitches to glide effortlessly across the needle. Flexible cords easily connect to the interchangeably tips and are kink and twist resistant. There really are no other needles like Karbonz!

We carry two different interchangeable sets as well as individual size needle tips in two lengths. We have a full selection of different cord lengths that can be used with any Knitter's Pride interchangeable needles.

Karbonz Interchangeable Circular Needle Set - Deluxe

This set contains 2 each of the following 4.5" interchangeable tips: US 2.5 (3.0mm), US 3 (3.25mm), US 4 (3.5mm), US 5 (3.75mm), US 6 (4.0mm), US 7 (4.5mm), US 8 (5.0mm), US 9 (5.5mm), US 10 (6.0mm); 4 cords to make 24", (2) 32", and 40" needles; 1 set of size markers; 8 end caps; 4 cord keys; 1 cord case; 1 fabric zip case.

Karbonz Interchangeable Circular Needle Set - Special

This set contains 2 each of the following 3.5" interchangeable tips: US 4 (3.5mm), US 5 (3.75mm), US 6 (4.0mm), US 7 (4.5mm), US 8 (5.0mm), US 9 (5.5mm), US 10 (6.0mm); 2 cords to make 16" needles; 1 set of size markers; 4 end caps; 2 cord keys; 1 black fabric case.
