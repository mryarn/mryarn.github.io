About

Mr. Yarn

Mr. Yarn started just as a passing thought all the way back in 2007, and it is hard to believe that my dream has finally come to fruition. I have designed and created every aspect of the site with a great amount of help from my husband, and has very much been a labor of love throughout the years. We’re glad to finally be sharing all our hard work with you, and hope you enjoy it as much as we do. Mr. Yarn will always aim to offer the best possible yarn shopping experience with a beautiful selection of yarns at affordable prices.

Grace

Some of you may already know me as GraceIvy on Ravelry or KathrynIvy.com, the knitting blog that I run with my sister Alice. We started KathrynIvy in 2006, as fairly new knitters, and since then we’ve really grown both in our experience and commitment to knitting. It was Alice who taught me how to knit and she also introduced me to Malabrigo. She has been an invaluable help to getting Mr. Yarn up and running, as well as an incredible source of inspiration. Our shared passion for knitting has kept us close despite living thousands of miles apart.
